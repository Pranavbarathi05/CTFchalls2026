# Formality Breach - Description

## Challenge Overview

You receive an anonymous tip about a company that's collecting customer feedback through what appears to be a legitimate form. The form accepts responses and even allows you to edit your submissions... but something doesn't feel right.

Your mission: Investigate this form and find what's really going on.

---

## What You're Given

- A URL to a customer feedback form
- The form appears to work normally
- You can submit responses
- You can edit your responses unlimited times
- But... are they actually recording anything?

---

## Your Task

Investigate the form application and discover the hidden information. The flag is somewhere in the analytics...

**Starting Point:** http://localhost:8015

---

## Hints

- Forms that let you edit infinitely are suspicious
- Google Forms has a specific URL pattern for viewing analytics
- Not everything you see is what it seems
- Sometimes the frontend is just for show

---

## Skills Required

- Basic web navigation
- URL manipulation
- Critical thinking
- Pattern recognition

---

## Difficulty: Easy

This is a beginner-friendly challenge focused on:
- Web reconnaissance
- Understanding frontend vs backend
- Common web application patterns
- Information disclosure vulnerabilities
